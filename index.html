My dearest Anne,

Today you turn 21. Twenty-one years of the world having you in it — and somehow, impossibly, I get to be the one standing closest to you on this day. I don't take that lightly. Not even for a second.

It started on January 15th, this year. That one day changed everything for me. The moment you said yes — the moment you let me in — something inside me settled in a way it never had before. Like I had been searching for something without knowing what it was, and then there you were, and I understood completely.

I want you to know what it feels like to love you. It feels like waking up and having something to look forward to. It feels like every ordinary Tuesday being special just because I get to text you good morning. It feels like laughing too hard at stupid things and not caring because you're the one laughing with me. It feels like peace. Real, quiet, beautiful peace. Loving you, Anne, is the easiest thing I have ever done in my life.

You are funnier than you think you are. You are stronger than you give yourself credit for. You have this way of existing in a room that makes everything warmer, quieter, and more alive all at the same time. I don't know how you do it. I just know I never want to stop being around it. I never want to stop being around you.

I think about all the things that had to go exactly right for us to end up here — and I am grateful for every single one of them. Every detour, every delayed moment, every small coincidence that pointed us toward each other. I wouldn't change any of it. Not one thing.

21 is just the beginning, baby. The beginning of you stepping fully into who you are. The beginning of all the big, beautiful things that are waiting for you. And I want to be there — right there, beside you — for every single step of it. For the celebrations and the hard days. For the adventures and the quiet evenings. For all of it, for as long as you'll have me.

This page, these games, these words — none of it comes close to what I actually feel. But I hope it makes you smile. I hope it makes you feel, even for a moment, just how completely and thoroughly you are loved by me.

Happy 21st Birthday, Anne Kabby. You deserve every good thing this world has to give. And I am going to spend as long as you will let me reminding you of exactly that.

All of my love, forever —
Ray 💜
